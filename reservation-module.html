Reservation Module

Staying in touch with your customers even after they’ve availed your product or service is essential in building a brand and helps in customer retention. Did you know that repeat customers can be attributed to a brand’s success at multiple levels? Have you ever felt you need to do more to keep every customer updated on what’s happening?

From storing all data under one roof to segregating it with various parameters in place of your business needs and assembling analytics for future use - Delicon Reservation System is the robust tool that brands and businesses need to establish a niche for themselves.


Why do u need such a module


  • Unorganized Customer Details

    • Hundreds of customers, thousands of orders, and choices even more. Mind-boggling, isn’t it? Not anymore when you can stack all neatly based on your convenience in a storage system always within reach.

  • Impossible To Follow Up

    • Want to send a wish of your customer’s special day? Or want to send updates to specific customers about a product or service you’ve just launched? Few clicks and you can do what you desire.

  • Fragile Customer Relationship

    • There are many hotels across the town serving brilliant food and brew. But why should the customer choose you over more? Get more personal with them and delight with offers they just cannot resist.

  • Lack Of Digitized Analytics

    • Data and decision making go hand in hand. Make decisions for the future of your business based on the customer data, choices, and analytics that DRS compiles and provides to make informed choices.

  • Too Much Competition

    • It is a matter of now or future. Because some time or the other, a streamlining system is what every brand would seek. Act now, adopt now and stay ahead of your competition selling more, scaling faster.

  • Manual Errors

    • Errors can be fatal and may even lead to massive losses or, sometimes, close to an organization. Take no chances by having an automated system that does the tasks while you focus on propelling your business.
